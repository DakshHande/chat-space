About ChatSpace

Our Mission

Welcome to ChatSpace! Our goal is to create a safe, open, and friendly environment for people to connect, share ideas, and enjoy casual conversations in a welcoming atmosphere. ChatSpace believes in the power of human connection and works to maintain a space where people from all walks of life can chat freely and respectfully.

Our Values

  • Respect: We promote respectful interactions between all users and encourage kindness and consideration in every conversation.
  • Privacy: We strive to keep conversations anonymous and to protect user identities. Your interactions are stored only on your local device, ensuring that your privacy is respected.
  • Freedom: ChatSpace is a place for open communication and freedom of expression, as long as all users follow our community guidelines.

Community Guidelines

We want ChatSpace to be a safe and enjoyable place for everyone, so please follow these guidelines:

  • Avoid using vulgar or offensive language. Messages containing such words will lead to automatic restriction from the chat.
  • Do not post any links or promotional content. Our system filters and restricts any messages with external links.
  • Engage in friendly and constructive conversations. Abusive behavior towards other users is strictly prohibited.
  • Any violations of the guidelines will result in temporary or permanent bans.
