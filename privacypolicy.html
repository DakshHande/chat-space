Privacy Policy

Introduction

At ChatSpace, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information and what measures we take to safeguard it.

No User Registration

ChatSpace is a completely free platform. We do not require user sign-ups or login credentials. You can access the chat anonymously without providing any personal information.

Data Collection

Since we do not require sign-ups, we do not collect any personal data or user credentials. The only data we may gather is non-personal information, such as your IP address, for the purpose of maintaining security and managing user access.

Cost of Running the Website

ChatSpace operates as a free service. We do not accept donations or financial contributions. Our costs are managed through minimal advertising and personal investment from our team to keep the platform running smoothly.

Rules and Regulations

To ensure a safe and respectful environment, we have established the following rules for using the chat:

  • No use of offensive language or slurs.
  • No sharing of personal information.
  • Do not post any adult content or inappropriate links.
  • Respect other users and their opinions.
  • Engage in constructive conversations; avoid spamming.
  • Users violating these rules may face temporary or permanent bans.

Security Measures

While we strive to maintain a safe environment, users are encouraged to take their own precautions. Do not share sensitive information and report any suspicious behavior to our support team.

By using ChatSpace, you agree to our Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time. Please check back periodically for updates.
