Contact Us

We're here to assist you! If you are experiencing any issues or have questions, please feel free to reach out to us via email. Your feedback and concerns are important to us!

Contact Email:

support@chatspace.com

Please use the above email address for all inquiries, including:

  • Issues related to chat bans.
  • Technical difficulties while using the platform.
  • General queries or feedback regarding ChatSpace.
  • Suggestions for improving our chat system.

How to Reach Out:

  • Make sure to provide a clear subject line that summarizes your issue or query.
  • In the body of the email, be as descriptive as possible about the issue you're facing.
  • Include your username (if applicable) and any relevant details that could help us assist you better.
  • To ensure a prompt response, please avoid sending multiple emails for the same issue.

Thank you for your patience and understanding. We aim to respond to all emails within 24-48 hours!
